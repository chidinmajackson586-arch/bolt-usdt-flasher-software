Frequently Asked Questions

What is a flash transaction? ▼

A flash transaction is a temporary cryptocurrency transfer that allows users to move digital assets across blockchains instantly. It's perfect for testing, arbitrage, and quick settlements.

Is Bolt Flasher secure? ▼

Yes, we use military-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and advanced security protocols to protect your account and transactions. Your funds are always secured.

What cryptocurrencies are supported? ▼

We support USDT, Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, TRON, and many other major cryptocurrencies on multiple blockchains including Ethereum, Bitcoin, BSC, Tron, and Polygon.

How long does a transaction take? ▼

Most transactions are completed in seconds. The exact time depends on network congestion and blockchain confirmation times, typically ranging from 10 seconds to 5 minutes.

What are your fees? ▼

Our fees start at a competitive 2% per transaction for most users. Premium users enjoy lower rates. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom pricing.

Is there a minimum transaction amount? ▼

No minimum amount required. You can flash any amount, from small test transactions to large institutional transfers.
